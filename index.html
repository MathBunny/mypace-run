VDOT Equivalents
VDOT is a Daniels-style fitness estimate derived from a race result. Selecting a race treats your current pace as that race effort, then back-solves equivalent performances for the others.
Choose a race below to treat the current input as that race pace.

Grade Adjusted Pace
GAP estimates what your effort on an uphill or downhill grade would feel like on flat ground. It is best used as a practical training comparison, not a perfect physiological model.
Adjust the grade to see the equivalent flat pace across units.
